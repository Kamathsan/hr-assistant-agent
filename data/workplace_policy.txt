ROOMAN TECHNOLOGIES - WORKPLACE POLICIES & CODE OF CONDUCT

=== WORKING HOURS ===
Standard working hours: 9:30 AM - 6:30 PM (Monday to Friday)
Flexible timing: Core hours 11 AM - 4 PM (mandatory presence)
Lunch break: 1 hour (between 12:30 PM - 2:30 PM)
Week off: Saturday and Sunday
Total working hours: 40 hours per week

=== ATTENDANCE POLICY ===
Grace period: 15 minutes (max 3 times per month)
Late arrival: Beyond grace period requires manager approval
Half-day: Working less than 4 hours
Full-day absence: Marked if not informed within 2 hours
Biometric/HRMS: Mandatory attendance marking for all employees

=== PROBATION PERIOD ===
Duration: 6 months for all new hires
Performance review: At 3 months and 6 months
Confirmation: Based on satisfactory performance evaluation
Notice period during probation: 30 days or 1 month salary
Can be extended by 3 months if performance needs improvement

=== NOTICE PERIOD ===
Standard notice: 60 days for confirmed employees
Senior roles (Manager+): 90 days
During probation: 30 days
Buyout option: Available at company's discretion (1-2 months salary)
Serving notice: Full attendance required unless on approved leave

=== RESIGNATION PROCESS ===
1. Submit formal resignation letter to reporting manager
2. HR initiates exit formalities
3. Knowledge transfer to designated team member
4. Asset return: Laptop, ID card, access cards, documents
5. Exit interview with HR
6. Full and Final settlement within 45 days
7. Relieving letter and experience certificate issued

=== DRESS CODE ===
Business casual attire on regular days
Formals required for client meetings
Friday casual: Jeans and t-shirts allowed
No offensive/inappropriate clothing
Footwear: Closed-toe shoes preferred (no flip-flops)
Personal hygiene and grooming expected

=== REMOTE WORK POLICY ===
Hybrid model: 2 days WFH + 3 days office (post-probation)
Full remote: Only for special circumstances with VP approval
Productivity tracking: Daily standups and task updates
Equipment: Company laptop, VPN access provided
Internet: ₹1,000 monthly reimbursement
Emergency WFH: Approved on case-by-case basis

=== CODE OF CONDUCT ===
Respect and dignity for all colleagues
Zero tolerance for harassment, discrimination, bullying
Professional behavior in all communications
Confidentiality of company information
No moonlighting or conflict of interest
Prohibition of alcohol/drugs on premises
Social media: No posting confidential company information

=== ANTI-HARASSMENT POLICY ===
Zero tolerance for sexual harassment, verbal abuse, intimidation
Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) available
Anonymous reporting mechanism via ethics@rooman.net
Protection against retaliation for complainants
Mandatory sensitivity training for all employees
Strict action including termination for proven cases

=== INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ===
All work created during employment belongs to company
Inventions, code, designs: Company property
Non-disclosure of proprietary information
Non-compete: 1 year for senior positions
Client information: Strictly confidential

=== DATA SECURITY & IT POLICY ===
Strong passwords: Minimum 12 characters, changed every 90 days
Two-factor authentication: Mandatory for all systems
Email security: No opening suspicious attachments
Device encryption: Full disk encryption on all company devices
No unauthorized software installation
VPN mandatory for remote access
Report security incidents immediately to IT team

=== TRAVEL POLICY ===
Domestic travel: Economy class flights, AC 2-tier trains
International travel: Economy class (Business class for 10+ hours)
Hotel accommodation: Up to ₹4,000/night (metro), ₹3,000/night (other cities)
Daily allowance: ₹1,500 (domestic), $50 (international)
Advance: 70% of estimated expense before travel
Reimbursement: Within 7 days of return with bills

=== EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT ===
Submit claims within 15 days of expense
Approval required from manager
Original bills mandatory (GST invoices preferred)
Payment processed in next salary cycle
Categories: Travel, client entertainment, team events, training

=== PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT ===
Annual appraisal cycle: April
Mid-year review: October
Rating scale: 1-5 (1=Needs improvement, 5=Outstanding)
360-degree feedback: From peers, managers, subordinates
Individual Development Plan (IDP) creation
Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) for underperformers

=== GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL ===
Open-door policy with managers and HR
Formal grievance: Submit to hr@rooman.net
Response time: Within 7 working days
Escalation: CHRO if not resolved
Anonymous helpline: 1800-XXX-XXXX

=== OFFICE FACILITIES ===
Cafeteria: Subsidized breakfast and lunch
Pantry: Coffee, tea, snacks available
Gaming zone: Foosball, TT, PS5 for breaks
Library: Technical books and magazines
Parking: Free parking for all employees
Shuttle service: From major metro stations
