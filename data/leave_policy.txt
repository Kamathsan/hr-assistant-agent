ROOMAN TECHNOLOGIES - LEAVE POLICY 2024

=== ANNUAL LEAVE ===
All full-time employees are entitled to 24 days of paid annual leave per year.
Leave accrues at 2 days per month of service.
Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 12 days to the next calendar year.
Leave requests must be submitted at least 5 working days in advance for approval.
During probation period (first 6 months), employees can avail up to 6 days of leave.

=== SICK LEAVE ===
Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per year.
Sick leave does not accumulate year over year.
For sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days, a medical certificate is mandatory.
Sick leave can be availed on short notice but must be intimated to the manager within 2 hours of shift start.

=== CASUAL LEAVE ===
10 days of casual leave are provided per year.
Casual leave cannot be carried forward to the next year.
Can be combined with annual leave for extended breaks.
Minimum notice period: 1 day in advance (except emergencies).

=== MATERNITY LEAVE ===
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks (182 days) of paid maternity leave.
Can be availed from 8 weeks before expected delivery date.
Adoptive mothers are entitled to 12 weeks of leave from the date of adoption.
Employees must provide medical documentation from a registered practitioner.

=== PATERNITY LEAVE ===
Male employees are entitled to 15 days of paid paternity leave.
Must be availed within 6 months of child's birth or adoption.
Requires submission of birth certificate or adoption papers.

=== BEREAVEMENT LEAVE ===
5 days of paid leave for immediate family (parents, spouse, children, siblings).
3 days of paid leave for extended family (grandparents, in-laws).
Can be extended by using annual or casual leave.

=== WORK FROM HOME POLICY ===
Employees can request up to 2 WFH days per week after probation.
Must be approved by reporting manager 24 hours in advance.
Not applicable during critical project milestones or client meetings.
Full-time remote work requires VP approval and special circumstances.

=== SABBATICAL LEAVE ===
Employees with 5+ years of service can apply for unpaid sabbatical leave.
Maximum duration: 6 months.
Requires 3 months advance notice and department head approval.
Job position is not guaranteed upon return but equivalent role will be offered.

=== LEAVE APPROVAL PROCESS ===
1. Submit leave request via HRMS portal
2. Manager reviews and approves/rejects within 48 hours
3. HR team receives automatic notification
4. Employee gets email confirmation
5. Leave balance is updated in the system

=== LEAVE ENCASHMENT ===
Employees can encash up to 15 days of accumulated annual leave per year.
Encashment requests must be made during annual appraisal cycle.
Payment will be processed with the next salary cycle.
